About RetrofitList.ie

Ireland's dedicated home retrofit directory and resource hub — making it easier to find the right professional and understand your options.

Our Purpose

RetrofitList.ie was built to solve a simple but frustrating problem: finding trustworthy retrofit professionals in Ireland is harder than it should be. Homeowners often don't know where to start, what questions to ask, or how to navigate the grant system — and contractors struggle to reach homeowners who are genuinely ready to invest in their homes.

We bridge that gap. Our directory brings together BER assessors, insulation contractors, heat pump installers, solar PV companies, window and door specialists, ventilation experts, and one-stop-shop retrofit providers — all in one searchable, filterable resource.

Alongside the directory, our News & Guides section keeps homeowners up to date on SEAI grant changes, local authority schemes, planning regulations, and practical how-to information. We aim to be the most useful single resource for anyone thinking about retrofitting their home in Ireland.

Who We're For

Homeowners — whether you're just starting to think about energy upgrades or ready to get quotes, RetrofitList.ie helps you understand your options, find local professionals, and stay informed about grant funding.

Retrofit professionals — if you offer any retrofit-related service and want to be found by motivated homeowners across Ireland, we want to list you. See the section below for how to get started.

A Note on Accuracy

Our directory data is sourced directly from the SEAI contractor register — the same register SEAI uses to validate grant eligibility. That said, registrations can lapse, and we recommend homeowners independently verify that any contractor is currently active on the SEAI register before signing any agreement or making payment. The SEAI contractor register is freely searchable at seai.ie.

List Your Business

Getting listed on RetrofitList.ie puts your business in front of Irish homeowners who are actively researching energy upgrades. Listings include your company name, county, services, contact details, and a short description.

What you'll need to provide

  • Company name
  • County (where you are based or primarily operate)
  • Service categories (you can select multiple)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Website URL (optional but recommended)
  • Short description of your business (2–4 sentences)

To get listed, email your details to listings@RetrofitList.ie and we'll add your business to the directory within 2–3 working days.
